Phase 1: Skill Profile

Phase 1 is where we get a vision of who you are. In phase 1 we look at different skills and try to find and match you to a career that values skills and certifications that you have already developed. Though we match you with a career in this phase, the true goal is to help you think about what abilities you have already gained, and help you to understand your desires for a career in IT.

Phase 2: Choose a Path

In Phase 2 we take the careers that you've matched with as well as an IT sector that you can fit into, and we let you explore. The goal is to help you understand the different careers including the time required to succeed and average salaries in this career. If you would like to explore the sectors and choose a path without being matched you can also click the explore Sectors tab up above.
