Phase 1: Skill Profile

Phase 1 is where we get a vision of who you are. In phase 1 we look at different skills and try to find and match you to a career that values skills and certifications that you have already developed. Though we match you with a career in this phase, the true goal is to help you think about what abilities you have already gained, and help you to understand your desires for a career in IT.

Phase 2: Choose a Path

In Phase 2 we take the careers that you've matched with as well as an IT sector that you can fit into, and we let you explore. The goal is to help you understand the different careers including the time required to succeed and average salaries in this career. If you would like to explore the sectors and choose a path without being matched you can also click the explore Sectors tab up above.

Phase 3: Effort Required

The goal of phase 3 is to take a chosen career path and find out what you need to complete to become proficient in the field. This includes a list of the most common certifications with links to access these certifications. We also show stories of veterans who have made their way into tech giving real world examples of what a job in Tech can mean for you and your family!

Phase 4: The Big Picture

Phase 4 is where the roadmap shows its fullest value. In phase 4 you can see how different careers relate to each other and how work into eachother. This phase gives a strong overview of the different areas within IT, and how one career choice can lead to further learning and higher career paths in the future. This helps show how the certificates you've already gained can be used to find higher salary careers as well as advance within the IT community.
